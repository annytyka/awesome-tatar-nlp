## Morphology

* ??? Apertium project https://wiki.apertium.org/wiki/Turkic_languages maintains a large number of morphological analysers for the Turkic languages. These analysers are used, e.g. in annotation of the Corpus of Written Tatar among other things. There are also two small, unreleased treebanks of Tuvan and Crimean Tatar available along with the analysers.

## Datasets

annotated

* National corpus https://tugantel.tatar/ The volume of the Corpus is 180,000,000 tokens (by December, 2018). The Corpus contains texts of different styles and genres (fiction, media texts, official documents, educational and scientific literature, etc.).
* Written corpus https://www.corpus.tatar/ This corpus contains a Text Corpus of the modern Tatar language consisting of over 500 million word occurrences (>620 mln tokens).
* Tatar Belletristic Literature Corpus https://litcorpus.antat.ru/ The corpus includes prosaic and poetic works of Tatar authors, texts of particular folklore genres, as well as works translated from other languages into Tatar.
* Tatar-Russian Parallel Corpus https://huggingface.co/datasets/yasalma/tt-ru-language-corpus
* ??? datasets https://github.com/tat-nlp/SART https://arxiv.org/pdf/1904.00365
* Tatar-English Parallel Corpus https://huggingface.co/datasets/yasalma/tt-en-language-corpus
* Tatar Speech Corpus ASR https://huggingface.co/datasets/yasalma/TatSC_ASR https://github.com/IS2AI/Soyle

* Tatar Instruction-Following Dataset https://huggingface.co/datasets/romgor/tatar-instruction-dataset-small
